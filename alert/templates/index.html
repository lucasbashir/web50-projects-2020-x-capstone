About Crime Alert


It is our believe that combatting crime in our community is a collective resposibility.

Crime is systemic and should be tackled using a systemic approach where everyone plays an

active role in fighting it.

This website gives all citizens the privilege to report crimes in their vicinity

Five ways to stay away from crime:

  1. Stay aware of your surroundings and trust your instincts.
  2. Keep your doors and windows locked, and don't open them for strangers.
  3. Avoid walking alone in unsafe areas, especially at night.
  4. Don't carry large amounts of cash or valuables with you.
  5. Get involved in your community and support crime prevention programs.
